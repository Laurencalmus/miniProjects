Fizz buzz

FizzBuzz is a program that prints the numbers from 1 to 100, but prints "Fizz" for multiples of three instead of the number and the word “Buzz” instead of the number for the multiples of five. For numbers which are multiples of both three and five “FizzBuzz” is printed.
